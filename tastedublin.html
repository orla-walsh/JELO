Taste of Dublin

Finest Dining

Taste of Dublin is Ireland's premier food and drinks festival. A four day festival of indulgence with the city's hottest restaurants showcasing exquisite signature dishes. Visitors have the chance to meet award-winning chefs and top-notch foodies while enjoying the stunning backdrops of the Iveagh Gardens

Dates & Prices

The festival has an afternoon and evening session, lasting approximately 4-5 hours, allowing you to browse atrisan stalls and enjoy live entertainment. Tickets are sold each session for that time and and date. Starting at only €4pp, it is a must attend event!!

What To Expect

The event is a great excuse to get together with friends & family and enjoy a relaxing and entertaining day out

Leading Chefs

Watch as world class chefs work their magic in live demonstrations. Sample signature dishes from over 20 of Dublin's most highly rated restaurants

Taste Experiences

Indulge in complimentary activities such as cooking masterclasses, whiskey and craft beer tastings and food and wine pairings

Taste Marketplace

Sample boutique food markets where a mouth-watering array of more than 60 food and drink purveyors, artisan producers and manufacturers offer exclusive and unique products

Tickets can be bought directly from the Taste of Dublin website
